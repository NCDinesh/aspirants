# IELTS vs PTE: Which Test is Right for You?

Choosing the right English proficiency test is essential for your study abroad journey. Both **IELTS** and **PTE** are widely accepted, but they differ in structure, scoring, and delivery format.

## Key Differences

- **IELTS**: Available in both paper-based and computer-based formats. It includes Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking sections, with a face-to-face Speaking test.
- **PTE**: Fully computer-based with AI scoring. The Speaking part is also done on a computer.

## Which Should You Choose?

- **Comfortable with face-to-face interviews?** Go for IELTS.
- **Prefer computer-based exams with quick results?** PTE might suit you.

---

## Final Thoughts

Both are great options for proving your English skills. Choose based on your preference and university requirements.
